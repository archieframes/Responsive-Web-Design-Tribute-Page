Dr. John

A photo of Dr. John in his performance attire, taken circa 1970 by Michael Ochs.

Dr. John, born Malcolm John Rebennack Jr. on November 21st, 1940 in New Orleans, was a well-known and very active musician operating across a variety of genres from the late 1950s till the time of his death, June 6th, 2019. He is best known for his extravagant stage performances, which incorporated themes of voodoo and his 1973 Top-10 hit single, "Right Place, Wrong Time." You can learn more about Dr. John by visiting The Official website of Dr. John and the Nite Trippers.
